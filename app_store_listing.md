## English Listing

### Title (30 chars max)

### Subtitle (30 chars max)
Group dining decision made easy

### Full Description

Can't decide where to eat with friends? Farketmez turns group venue selection into a fun, fast-paced voting game.

**How It Works**

1. Create a Room — Open a room in seconds and share the code with your friends
2. Join Together — Everyone joins the same room and sees nearby venues
3. Vote — Like or skip venues with your allocated votes (3–7 per round)
4. Decide — The venue with the most votes wins — everyone leaves happy

**Features**

✅ Real-time sync — votes update instantly for all participants
📍 Location-based suggestions — top restaurants and cafes near you via Google Places
⭐ Scoring system — predict the winning venue and earn points
🗺️ Map view — explore venues on an interactive map
📸 Venue photos — real photos for every place
🕐 Open/Closed status — filter to only open venues
💰 Price range — find places that fit your budget
📜 Room history — review past rooms and winning venues
🔔 Push notifications — get notified when friends join your room

**Why Farketmez?**

Group decisions shouldn't take forever. Farketmez solves the "where should we go?" problem in seconds. With location-based recommendations, real-time voting, and a fun scoring system, the debate ends here.

Farketmez: Let's decide where to go!
